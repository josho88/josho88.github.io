What we do

We look at the bigger picture. SVA provide analytics and actuarial services to help drive better financial decision making. Specialising in the insurance sector, we have extensive experience in pricing, predictive modelling, financial modelling, managing data and building bespoke visualisation tools.

SVA are passionate about technology and programming - our current interests include text analytics, GLM's, building web apps with R 'shiny' and applied machine learning. We use technologies like R, Python, SQL, h2o.ai, shiny, tensorflow and many more to deliver end to end analytics solutions for businesses.
